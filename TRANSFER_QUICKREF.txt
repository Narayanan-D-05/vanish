🔐 PRIVACY vs REGULAR TRANSFERS:

Regular Transfer (Public)
├─ Command: transfer 0.0.123456 10
├─ Speed: ⚡ 3 seconds
├─ Privacy: ❌ Public on ledger
└─ Use for: Quick payments, no privacy needed

Privacy Transfer (ZK-Proof)
├─ Command: shield 50 → Pool → Withdraw
├─ Speed: ⏱️ 5-30 minutes (batching)
├─ Privacy: ✅ Zero-knowledge proofs
└─ Use for: Financial privacy, breaking links
